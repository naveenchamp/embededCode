India vs England 3rd Test Day1:

"England Tour Of india"

India vs England, 3rd Test Day 1, IND vs ENG:Rohit Sharma and Ravindra Jadeja slammed centuries. Sarfaraz Khan was run out after brilliant 50. Indian captain Rohit Sharma reached a magnificent century during the third session of the opening day in Rajkot, strengthening the Indian innings alongside Ravindra Jadeja. Rohit and Jadeja slammed centuries as the duo added 204 runs to stage India's fightback. Notably, Jadeja reached his fourth Test ton at his home ground while Rohit notched up his first century in 11 innings. Making a spectacular debut for India, Sarfaraz Khan slammed his first half-century in 48 balls as Rohit and Co. reached 326/5 at Stumps on Day 1 in Rajkot.
